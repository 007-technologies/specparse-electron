Welcome to SpecParse

SpecParse turns a CSI MasterFormat spec PDF into a Procore-compatible submittal log in under two minutes. Here's the three-step flow:

  1. 01
    Upload Drop a spec PDF or browse to one. SpecParse reads CSI MasterFormat books — both 1995 (5-digit) and 2004+ (6-digit) numbering.
  2. 02
    Process SpecParse identifies submittals across the spec. ~55 common sections come straight from a curated dictionary (verified, no AI guess); everything else routes through Claude Haiku.
  3. 03
    Review & export Confidence flags (green / yellow / red) tell you which rows came from the dictionary vs AI. Edit, reorder, or remove rows, then export an Excel file ready to drag into Procore's Submittals importer.
